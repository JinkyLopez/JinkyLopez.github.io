Premium Arabica Beans

Grown amidst the pristine mountains of the Philippines, these coffee beans are meticulously cultivated at high altitudes, where the cool climate and fertile soil impart a unique richness and depth to every cup. With notes of caramel, chocolate, and hints of fruit, each sip is a journey of sensory delight, showcasing the craftsmanship and dedication of generations of local farmers. Elevate your coffee experience and savor the essence of Kapangan's finest Arabica beans, a true testament to the artistry of Philippine coffee culture.

Roasted to Perfection

Our beans are lovingly hand-roasted outdoors, allowing the gentle kiss of wood smoke to impart a depth of flavor and complexity that is unmatched. With each batch carefully tended to by skilled artisans, our wood-fired roasting process captures the essence of our rich coffee heritage, delivering a truly exceptional brew that embodies the soul of Kapangan's coffee culture.

Shipping Worldwide

We ship worldwide to coffee enthusiasts everywhere! From the lush mountains of the Philippines to your doorstep, each bean carries the essence of our rich coffee heritage, crafted to deliver a premium coffee experience like no other. Whether you're in the bustling streets of Manile or to the other side of the Earth, let the aroma and taste of our wood-roasted Arabica beans transport you to the heart of Kapangan's coffee culture, no matter where in the world you may be.

Quality Assurance

Our coffee proudly bears certifications such as organic, ensuring that each bean is grown without the use of harmful chemicals, promoting environmental sustainability and preserving the natural integrity of the land. Additionally, our commitment to fair trade practices guarantees that our farmers receive fair compensation for their hard work, empowering local communities and fostering economic development. With a dedication to quality assurance at every step of the process, our Arabica coffee support a more equitable and sustainable coffee industry.
